Group Reflection

We as a group feel we didn’t actually start our project until far too late making it rather stressful to sort everything out such as who is doing what and timeframes of work completion leading to a mad dash to try get the work done to a high standard.

In fact, starting on it earlier would’ve made us get the hang of communicating using our 7desired platform quicker leading to a more open dialogue and being more collaborative and being able to provide more detailed responses to the questions asked on the specification as more minds thinking on everything means more ideas and different viewpoints. Delegations of roles also should've been created earlier in order to stay organised and up to date.

Our line of communication was rather lackluster and wasn’t very responsive leading to a lot of stress and confusion over the status of certain sections of the project getting completed on time.

Once we did get started however, I feel our group individually did all our work quite efficiently and to a good standard even though it was pushed out so quickly. We used what little time we had left efficiently, especially seeing as most group members also had other commitments and group assignments in this time frame. Our group members were very willing to help each other out with any tasks that needed to be completed. We also had frequent online meetings that allowed us to come together, discuss the assignment and highlight any concerns we had.

In the future, we aim to set group goals which should be achieved by the end of each week before the next meeting. This can allow for the divided tasks to be finished early, so there is enough time to polish and so members are able to move on to help others in need.

Something some of the group members found surprising was that we did not choose a group leader at any point throughout the whole assignment, A leadership role could have guided us, as they could have done a better job delegating tasks among all group members. A group leader would have also helped us keep perspective on the deadline and helped make us informed decisions.

All the group members contributed equally in the assignment, however there were some delays as some tasks needed to be read clearly so they could have been split amongst more members making it more fair. This occurred for the IT technologies section as it was originally given to 2 members which was a much greater task than we anticipated, which was then distributed to 2 more members.
